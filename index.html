About Me

Your Bridge Between Raw Data & Real Results

I'm Israel Mayokun — a Data Analyst, Business Analyst, and dedicated instructor with a passion for making data science accessible and actionable. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, I work with individuals and organisations to build real-world data skills.

Whether you're a beginner stepping into the world of spreadsheets, or a professional looking to harness the power of Power BI dashboards or AI automation, I provide structured, hands-on training designed to fast-track your growth.

My teaching philosophy: no fluff, no theory overload — just practical skills that you can apply from day one.
